Seamless

1 Jan, 0001

Seamless is an image resizing application that leverages Firebase for persistent storage of a users images. The name Seamless is derived from the seam carving algorithm it uses for image resizing. The seam carving algorithm iterates over each pixel, and generates an energy value. The energy values are measured by the pixel’s contrast to other surrounding pixels. After the energy values are calculated, the algorithm will create a “seam” through the image, finding the lowest energy values either top to bottom, or left to right.
